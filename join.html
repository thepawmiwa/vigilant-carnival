Ridgeway County Recruitment

Enlist in
The Carnival

The Vigilant Carnival doesn't recruit the timid. We want the bold — those who see authority for what it is and refuse to bow.

What We Expect

Requirements
for Entry

  • ★

    Active in Ridgeway County

    You must be an active player in Ridgeway County. Inactive members serve no purpose to the carnival.

  • ★

    Loyalty to the Group

    No double allegiances. If you're with us, you're with us — not with law enforcement or any DOT-aligned faction.

  • ★

    Willingness to Act

    We don't take passengers. Members participate in operations, patrols, and group activities regularly.

  • ★

    Respect the Code

    We oppose authority — not each other. Internal respect and communication are non-negotiable inside the Carnival.

  • ★

    No Law Enforcement Background

    Former or current police characters are not welcome. The badge and the carnival cannot coexist.

The Hierarchy

Ranks of
The Carnival

Drifter Entry

New recruits. Proving their loyalty and learning how the Carnival operates. Every legend starts here.

Roustabout Member

Trusted members who've shown up consistently. Cleared for group operations and internal communications.

Performer Veteran

Veterans who lead by example. Known faces in the county. Assigned roles in coordinated actions.

Ringmaster Officer

Officers who coordinate operations and manage members. Speak for the Carnival and direct the show.

The Director Leadership

Founders and top leadership. They run the Carnival. Their word is law — the only law we follow.

The Pledge

The Carnival Oath

I join the Vigilant Carnival of my own free will.
I answer to no badge, no barrier, no permit.
I stand with my fellow members above all else.
I will disrupt, dissent, and never comply.
The county belongs to the people — and we are the people.

— Signed in defiance

Process

How to Enlist

01

Find Us In-Game

Locate a Carnival member or Ringmaster in Ridgeway County. Make yourself known — we notice who moves with purpose.

02

Pass the Vetting

A Ringmaster or above will speak with you. No forms. No applications. We judge character by presence — not paperwork.

03

Take the Oath

Say the words. Mean them. You're a Drifter now — prove you belong here and the Carnival will rise with you.
