Who We Are

Against Authority.
For the People.

The Vigilant Carnival is an anti-establishment faction operating within Ridgeway County — born from fury at the badge-wearing enforcers and the road-blocking bureaucrats who believe their uniform grants them power over others. We exist to disrupt, destabilize, and dismantle every system they've built to keep people in line.

We answer to no authority — not the police who patrol our streets itching for control, and certainly not the Department of Transportation, whose cones, barriers, and permits are nothing but tools of submission dressed up in orange vests.

Organized as a carnival of chaos, we operate through mayhem, solidarity, and spectacle. Every action we take is a performance. Every performance is a statement. And every statement sends the same message: order is over.

01

Anti-Police

We oppose the abuse of authority that the Ridgeway County police force represents. Badges don't grant power — people do, and we never gave ours.

02

Anti-D.O.T.

Roads are public. Movement is a right. The Department of Transportation's barriers and blockades are instruments of control — and we reject every one of them.

03

Community First

We protect our own. Members of the Vigilant Carnival look out for each other in Ridgeway County, coordinating, communicating, and never backing down.

04

Spectacular Defiance

Resistance is theater. We make noise. We make scenes. We make them remember that this county belongs to everyone — not just the ones with a badge.
