The Vigilant Carnival Platform

Our Issues

Ridgeway County is broken. Here is exactly what the Carnival stands against — and what we demand changes, now.

01 Arms & Rights

Legalize the Para 17
& All Restricted Arms

The county's ruling class has decided what weapons are permitted — and unsurprisingly, they've kept the most powerful tools for themselves and their enforcers. The Para 17 and every other restricted arm sit behind locked bureaucratic doors while the people are left defenseless against a state that grows bolder by the day.

This is not coincidence. This is strategy. An unarmed population is a controlled population. When tyrants hold the upper hand, there is no limit to what they will do with it — and history in this county has shown us exactly that.

Full legalization of the Para 17 and all restricted weapons. The people have the right to match the firepower of those who would oppress them.
02 D.O.T.

Abolish the D.O.T.
Leave Our Cars Alone

The Department of Transportation was never about safety. It was always about dominance. Every cone placed, every roadblock erected, every citation issued is another brick in the wall they're building around our freedom of movement. They regulate, restrict, and seize — and call it public service.

Our vehicles are our property. Our roads are public. The D.O.T. has no legitimate authority over either. Their entire apparatus — the inspectors, the permits, the impounds — is a racket designed to shake down ordinary people while the county elite drive freely without consequence.

Abolish the D.O.T. entirely. Dismantle the checkpoints. Return the roads to the people they were built for.
03 Militarization

Defund the National Guard

A paramilitary force patrolling civilian streets is not protection — it is occupation. The National Guard in Ridgeway County answers to no one, operates with unchecked authority, and exists for a single purpose: to crush resistance before it can organize. They are the county's iron fist, wrapped in a flag and given a budget.

Power-hungry and unaccountable, they are deployed not against threats — but against people. Against protests. Against anyone who dares push back. Every dollar that funds their weapons and their vehicles is a dollar taken from communities they patrol like conquered territory.

Defund and disband the National Guard's street operations. No paramilitary force belongs among the people of Ridgeway County.
04 Police Reform

Reform the Police.
No More Free Passes.

The Ridgeway County Police Department has operated without consequence for too long. Officers who abuse their authority face no real discipline. Complaints disappear. Investigations go nowhere. The badge has become a shield against accountability — and the people bear the cost of every misconduct that goes unpunished.

We are not asking for perfection. We are demanding basic accountability. Every officer must answer for their actions — to the public, not to an internal committee of their own colleagues. Transparency, civilian oversight, and real consequences for misconduct are not radical ideas. They are the bare minimum.

Full accountability for every officer. Civilian oversight with real authority. No more closed-door investigations. No more free passes.

The Carnival doesn't petition. The Carnival demands.
